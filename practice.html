ABOUT US

GET TO KNOW US

Pawthentic was born from a deep love for pets and a desire to create a one-stop haven for their needs. We saw the need for a place where pet parents could find everything—from quality pet essentials to expert grooming—while also giving homeless cats and dogs a second chance through adoption.

What started as a small idea grew into a passionate mission: to provide compassionate care, trusted products, and a loving community for pets and their humans. Every service we offer, from grooming to adoption, is driven by our dedication to animal well-being and responsible pet ownership.

At Pawthentic, we believe pets aren’t just companions—they’re family. And we’re here to make sure they get the love and care they truly deserve.

OUR COMMITMENT

At Pawthentic, our promise is simple: to provide the highest level of care, quality, and compassion for every pet and pet parent who visit us, shop with us, or trust us with their pets.

Compassionate Pet Care

Whether through expert grooming or finding loving homes for adoptable pets, we treat every cat and dog with kindness and respect.

Quality & Trust

Our products are carefully selected to ensure the best for your furry companions—because they deserve nothing less.

Responsible Pet Adoption

We’re committed to helping rescued pets find families who will love them for a lifetime.

Community & Education

We support responsible pet ownership through guidance, resources, and a welcoming space for all pet lovers.
